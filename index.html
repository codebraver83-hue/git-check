Welcome To Lebanon - The Land Of Cedars and History

Lebanon is a small country in the Middle East on the eastern Mediterranean shore, bordered by Syria and Palestine. Its strategic location has made it a crossroads of civilizations for millennia, contributing to a rich history and diverse culture. Officially the Lebanese Republic, its capital is Beirut and it is known as the "Land of the Cedars".

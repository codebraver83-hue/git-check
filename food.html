Lebanese Cuisine

Discover the flavors, colors, and traditions behind Lebanon's culinary heritage

Baba Ghanoush

It is a creamy, smoky dip made primarily from roasted or grilled eggplant, blended with tahini, garlic, lemon juice, and olive oil. It is often served as a side dish or dip with pita bread or vegetables. The dish has variations across the Middle East and can be garnished with parsley, pomegranate seeds, or nuts.

Tabbouleh

It is a fresh salad made from finely chopped parsley, tomatoes, mint, and onion, mixed with soaked bulgur wheat.

Warak Enab

It is a traditional dish of grape leaves stuffed with a mixture of rice, herbs, and often meat, then rolled and simmered in a lemon-flavored broth.

Labneh

It is a soft, tangy cheese made from strained yogurt. It has a thick, creamy texture and can be served as a dip or spread with toppings like olive oil and za'atar, or used in sandwiches and other dishes.

Kebbeh

It is a traditional dish made from a mixture of ground meat (often lamb, beef, or goat), fine bulgur wheat, and spices. It can be prepared in many ways, such as deep-fried balls stuffed with a spiced meat and nut filling, baked into a casserole, or served raw as a festive dish.

Shawarma

It is a popular Middle Eastern street food made of meat (such as chicken, beef, or lamb) stacked on a vertical rotisserie and cooked slowly.

Falafel

They are deep-fried balls or patties made from ground chickpeas or fava beans, mixed with herbs and spices. This popular street food is known for its crispy exterior and soft interior and is often served in a pita sandwich with toppings like tahini sauce, pickles, and fresh vegetables.

Manakeesh

It is a popular flatbread, particularly from Lebanon, that is often eaten for breakfast. It consists of a dough base topped with various ingredients like the herb and spice blend za'atar, cheese, or a mix of onions and tomatoes.

Sfeeha

It is a meat pie, often called lahm bi ajeen (lamb in dough), traditionally from the Levant region. It consists of a soft dough topped with a spiced mixture of ground meat (like lamb or beef), tomatoes, and onions. It can be made open-faced like a mini pizza or with the meat filling folded inside the dough.

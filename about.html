Discover our beautiful Lebanon

A land of history, culture, and natural beauty

Geography & Nature

  • Lebanon is the smallest country in the middle east, yet it has coastlines, mountains, and valleys all within 200 km.
  • The Cedars of Lebanon are 4000 years.
  • Lebanon has 4 distinct seasons so you can ski in the mountains in winter and swim in the Mediterranean in summer!

History

  • Lebanon was home to the Phonecians, the ancient seafarers who invented the first alphabet. Imagine that the letters you use today trace back here!
  • The city of Byblos id considered one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world, over 7000 years old.
  • Lebanon was the first Arab country to gain independence in 1943.
